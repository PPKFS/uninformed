Heart of the Sun is a room. The fish and the fowl are here.

To assay (name of kind of value K):
	repeat with item running through Ks:
		say "There's [item]."

Colour is a kind of value. The colours are red, blue and purple.

When play begins:
	say "People...";
	assay people;
	say "Things...";
	assay thing;
	say "Colours...";
	assay colours;
