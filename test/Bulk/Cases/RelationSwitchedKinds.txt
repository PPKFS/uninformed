Institute is a room. Zora is a woman in the institute. Chloe is a woman.

A language is a kind of value. The languages are English, Zubian, and Perplexish. 

Speaking relates one person to various languages. The verb to speak (he speaks, they speak, he spoke, 
it is spoken, he is speaking) implies the speaking relation.

Knowing relates one person to various people. The verb to know (he knows, they know, he knew, 
it is known, he is knowing) implies the knowing relation.

Zora speaks English. Zora knows Chloe.
