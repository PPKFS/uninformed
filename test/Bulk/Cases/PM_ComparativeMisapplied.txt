The Typographium is a room.

Weight is a kind of value. 100kg specifies a weight. A thing has a weight.
Definition: a thing is heavy if its weight is 50kg or more.

Height is a kind of value. 100cm specifies a height. A thing has a height.
Definition: a thing is tall if its height is 120cm or more.

When play begins:
	if the player is heavier than 70kg, say "Heavens to Betsy!";
	if the player is heavier than 70cm, say "Heavens to Betsy!"
