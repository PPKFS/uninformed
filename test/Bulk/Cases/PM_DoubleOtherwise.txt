Grocery is a room.

Fruit is a kind of thing.  Apple, banana, and cherry are fruits.

To say appearance of (F - a fruit):
   if F is an apple begin;
	 say "Apples are red.";
   otherwise if F is banana;
	say "Bananas are yellow.";
   otherwise;
           say "Cherries are red.";
   otherwise;
	 say "You can see a tasty fruit, [F] here.";
   end if;
