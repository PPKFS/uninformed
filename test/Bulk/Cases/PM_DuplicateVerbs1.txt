Frobonia is a room.

Frobbing relates one person to various things.

The verb to frob (he frobs, they frob, he frobbed, it is frobbed, he
is frobbing) implies the frobbing relation.

Mr Testy frobs the frobnitzim.  The printed name of the frobnitzim is
"frobnitzim (should be frobbed)".

Mopping relates one person to various things.

The verb to frob (he frobs, they frob, he frobbed, it is frobbed, he
is frobbing) implies the mopping relation.

The verb to be means the enclosure relation.
