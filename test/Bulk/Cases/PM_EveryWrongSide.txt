The plural of gentleman is gentlemen. A gentleman is a kind of man.

A blonde is a kind of woman. A brunette is a kind of woman.

Lorelei is a blonde. Dorothy is a brunette. Sir Francis is a gentleman.

Shipboard is a room. Lorelei, Dorothy and Sir Francis are in Shipboard.

Preference relates various people to various people.

The verb to prefer (he prefers, they prefer, he preferred, he is preferred)
implies the preference relation.

Every blonde prefers Sir Francis.
Sir Francis prefers every blonde.
