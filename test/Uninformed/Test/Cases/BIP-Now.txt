The tally is a number that varies.

A fruit is a kind of object. An apple, a pear and an orange are fruits. A fruit can be ripe or rotten.

To begin:
	showme the tally;
	now the tally is 7;
	showme the tally;
	showme whether or not the apple is ripe;
	showme whether or not the pear is ripe;
	showme whether or not the orange is ripe;
	now all the fruit is ripe;
	showme whether or not the apple is ripe;
	showme whether or not the pear is ripe;
	showme whether or not the orange is ripe;
