Workshop is a room.

Colour is a kind of value. The colours are red, green and white.

A colour can be bright, neutral or flat. Green is neutral.

When play begins:
	repeat with C running through colours:
		say "[C] is [colour condition of C].";
	now red is flat;
	repeat with C running through colours:
		say "[C] is [colour condition of C].";
	if red is flat, say "Red is flat.";
	otherwise say "Red isn't flat."
